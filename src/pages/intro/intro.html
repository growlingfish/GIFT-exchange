Gift

What is the Gift app?

Using this app you can add private digital messages to museum exhibits, and give a sequence of these "personalised exhibits" as a gift to another museum visitor.

Alternatively, you might have downloaded this app because you received an email to let you know that someone else sent you a gift.

What is a gift?

Each gift includes three personalised exhibits:

The Start - the first object that the gift recipient must find. The message added to this exhibit should give the experience a suitable beginning.

The Middle - this is the centre or heart of the gift: the filling in a sandwich. The message here might add to the story of the previous part, or not.

The End - this is where the gift is concluded: the message might be designed to leave the recipient in a particular mood or frame of mind.
