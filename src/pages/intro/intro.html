Gift

What is the Gift app?

Using this app you can add private digital messages to museum exhibits, and give a sequence of these "personalised exhibits" as gifts to another museum visitor.

What is a gift?

We suggest that you include three personalised exhibits in each gift:

The Start - the first object that you want to give. Add a message to this exhibit to give the experience a suitable beginning.

The Middle - this is the centre or heart of the gift: the filling in a sandwich. This could add to the story of the previous part, or not.

The End - this is where you conclude the gift: what frame of mind or mood do you want to create with the final part of the gift?

What is a gift?

When you have found exhibits to include in your gift, you need to capture them using the app:

A Photo (of the exhibit, to help the recipient recognise it)

Directions (to find the exhibit, what should the recipient do?)

A Location (which room or area is the exhibit in?)
