Introduction to Gift

Using this app you can add digital messages to museum exhibits, and give these gifts to other museum visitors.

Introduction to Gift

We suggest that you include three messages in each gift:

The Start - the first thing that you want the person who is receiving the gift to see. This might have popped out at you for them when you walked around the Museum or you searched a while for to give the experience a suitable beginning.

The Middle - this is the centre or heart of the gift, the filling of a sandwich. This could build from the previous part or not.

The End - this is where you want to conclude the gift, for your person to leave the experience in the Museum in the right frame of mind or mood.

Introduction to Gift

And in each part there are three tasks you need to make or unwrap in order to complete the Gift:

A Photo (of the object in the Museum you would like to visit)

Some Thoughts (on what it is about this object that connects it to them)

A Location (so that they can find it quite easily)

Introduction to Gift

So when you are ready to start, login and decide if you would like to Make a Gift for someone or Unwrap a Gift that someone has made for you.
